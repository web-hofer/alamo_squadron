Alamo Squadron Meetings
Alamo Squadron meets on the first Thursday of every month at Northside Ford of San Antonio: 12300 San Pedro Ave. Meetings are held in their conference and meeting room at the back of the showroom floor. Meetings are scheduled from 7:00 P.M. to 8:30PM. Visitors are always welcome and a family friendly environment is strictly maintained. A usual meeting consists of about 15-20 minutes of business, a Works-In-Progress review, a learning program/class, and a monthly contest among club members. No actual model building is conducted at our monthly meetings although there are many discussions and demonstrations around such subjects.
